About Me

Hello! I'm Achha Srishanth Rishi, a Computer Science Engineering student at Mahindra University with a deep passion for technology, creativity, and innovation. I’m driven by curiosity and love exploring how programming and artificial intelligence can be used to solve real-world challenges.

My academic background and projects have helped me develop a solid foundation in Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Machine Learning, and Web Development. I enjoy building practical applications — from developing games and face detection systems to signal-based machine learning models like SeizureSense.

Beyond coding, I’m an active learner who loves collaborating in tech communities such as the Hurricane Programming Club, where I also contributed to the club’s online presence through content and event promotions. I take pride in being both creative and analytical — combining technical knowledge with design tools like Canva and Adobe to bring ideas to life.

Outside the tech world, I enjoy playing cricket at a competitive level and playing the guitar to unwind and express creativity. I believe in consistent growth, teamwork, and using technology as a means to make a meaningful impact.
